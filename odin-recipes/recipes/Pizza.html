Pizza

Description

A classic homemade pizza with a crispy base, tangy sauce, and melted cheese. You can customize it with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup warm water
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • ½ cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • Vegetables (onion, capsicum, tomato, olives)
  • Oregano and chili flakes

Steps

  1. Activate yeast in warm water.
  2. Prepare and knead dough.
  3. Let it rise for 1 hour.
  4. Roll into base.
  5. Add sauce and toppings.
  6. Bake at 220°C for 12–15 minutes.
  7. Serve hot.
