Chocolate chips Muffins

Description

These chocolate chip muffins are quick and easy to make and simply delicious! The tops of these muffins have a crunchy sugar crust for a lovely contrast of texture in each bite.

Ingredientsx

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup milk
  • ½ cup melted butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F) and line a muffin tray with liners.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, brown sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, whisk milk, melted butter, eggs, and vanilla extract.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients gently.
  5. Fold in chocolate chips.
  6. Fill muffin cups about ¾ full.
  7. Bake for 18–22 minutes until golden brown.
  8. Let cool before serving.
