Mapple Coffee Cake

Description

A moist and flavorful cake infused with coffee and sweet maple syrup, often topped with a crumbly streusel. Perfect with tea or coffee.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons instant coffee
  • ¼ cup maple syrup
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • ¼ cup flour
  • 2 tablespoons butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Cream butter and sugar.
  3. Add eggs and mix.
  4. Add coffee, milk, and maple syrup.
  5. Mix dry ingredients and combine.
  6. Pour into pan.
  7. Add topping.
  8. Bake for 30–35 minutes.
  9. Cool and serve.
